Top 3 Movies

The Shawshank Redemption

A blend of both drama and action, this movie is a peronal favorite, a true classic movie. With a rating of 89% rotten tomatoes, along with 4.9 user ratings, this movie is a must watch classic movie that should not be ignored, being a movie worthy of the title of top classic movies.


The Usual Suspects

This is for sure a must-watch movie, being a classic and a hit anyways, whether with friends or family. It is a movie with 87% rotten tomatoes, a movie worth watching and enjoying. If you like thrillers and crime movies, this is the perfect movie to watch.


Top Gun: Maverick

This is a perfect movie to watch with one's family, especially on a big screen. Top Gun and its sequel are amazing must-watch movies, having great storylines, stunts, music, and more. If you haven't watched this movie, I suggest that you watch this movie. This movie had 96% rotten tomatoes, being a perfect movie about flying fighter jets and rookies on a mission.
